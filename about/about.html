Immigration is important to me because I have heard many stories of immigrants and how they got here. Many have lived in the U.S. for many years and have children born here. They are afraid of being separated from them.

My name is Emmanuel Ruiz, I currently live in Brooklyn, and my parents are both immigrants from Mexico.
